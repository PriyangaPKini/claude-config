# Workflow Practices

## Research Scope

Do not over-research — stop as soon as you have enough to proceed.

**Why:** Excessive exploration wastes context and time. Research should be purposeful and targeted, not open-ended.

**How to apply:** Before each additional research request, ask: "Do I already have enough context to answer the question or proceed with the task?" If yes, stop and move forward. Don't continue fetching/exploring out of thoroughness alone.
